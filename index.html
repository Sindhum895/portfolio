--------------

Road pothole detection refers to the technology and methods used to identify, locate, and often assess the severity of potholes on roads. Potholes are a significant issue for road infrastructure, causing safety hazards for drivers, damage to vehicles, and deterioration of roads over time. Pothole detection systems aim to address these problems by employing various technologies to detect and sometimes even predict the presence of potholes.
